How I Became Interested:

While I was attending the High School of Computers and Technology from 2016 to 2020 I had a teacher who was heavily into the stock market and philosophy. I picked up skills in investing and also an interest in philosophy, specifically Stoicism. In Stoicism there are the Big Three Stoics which are Seneca, Epictetus, and Marus Aurelius. In particular, I am most fascinated in the teachings of Marcus Aurelius's Meditations.

Stoicism Defined:

“Stoicism is focusing on things that are in your control, overcoming negative emotions, living in the present moment, 
helping others for the common good, and finding opportunity in every obstacle.” Phil Van Treuren.
